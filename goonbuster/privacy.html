Privacy Policy

GoonBuster · Silent Directive LLC · Effective July 8, 2026

The short version: your streak, your journal, your check-ins, and your quiz answers live on YOUR device. We do not have a database of your recovery data. There is no email, no real name, and no analytics tracker inside the app. The optional crew community stores exactly two things: the anonymous nickname you pick and the posts you choose to write. We cannot leak what we never collect.

What the app stores, and where

The GoonBuster app keeps everything it knows about you (streak start date, your "why," daily check-ins, journal entries, relapse notes, badges, settings) in your browser or phone's local storage, on your device. This data never leaves your device. We have no server that receives it, and no way to read it. Deleting the app data (or using "erase everything" in settings) removes it permanently.

The 60-second check (quiz)

Your quiz answers are scored inside your browser by the page itself. They are not transmitted, stored, or seen by us.

What we do NOT do

  • No email collection by the app or this site (the crew uses an anonymous nickname, nothing else)
  • No analytics or tracking pixels on the app
  • No selling, sharing, or transferring of personal data (we hold none to sell)
  • No notifications or messages unless you enable them on your device (reminders are generated locally)

What third parties handle

Payments and membership run through Stripe (stripe.com). When you start a trial or subscribe, Stripe collects your payment and account details under Stripe's own privacy policy and terms. We see membership status, not your card details.

The crew (in-app community)

The crew is optional. If you join, we store the nickname you pick, the posts and replies you write, and the streak-day number attached to them, on our server, so other members can see them. That is the complete list. No email, no real name, no journal, no check-ins, no location, no device tracking. Your nickname is not linked to your identity, and we recommend keeping it that way.

Every post has report and block controls. We remove content that breaks the rules (no links, no harassment) and can ban accounts that post it. "Leave the crew" in the app deletes your crew account and anonymizes your old posts. Questions or takedown requests: support@silentdirectivellc.com.

Referral tags: if you arrive through a partner link (for example a link with a source tag), this site remembers that tag in your own browser's local storage and passes it to Stripe at checkout so we know which partner sent you. It identifies the partner, not you.

Hosting: this site is served as static files by GitHub Pages, which may log standard technical request data (like IP addresses) as any web host does.

Age

GoonBuster is intended for users 18 and older. Purchasing a membership requires you to be at least 18.

Not medical advice

GoonBuster is a self-improvement tool, not medical care, therapy, or a substitute for professional help. If you are struggling, talking to a professional is a strong move, not a weak one.

Changes and contact

If this policy changes, the update appears on this page with a new effective date. Questions: support@silentdirectivellc.com.
