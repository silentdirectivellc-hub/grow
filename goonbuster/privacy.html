Privacy Policy

GoonBuster · Silent Directive LLC · Effective July 1, 2026

The short version: your streak, your journal, your check-ins, and your quiz answers live on YOUR device. We do not have a database of your recovery data. There is no account, no cloud sync, and no analytics tracker inside the app. We cannot leak what we never collect.

What the app stores, and where

The GoonBuster app keeps everything it knows about you (streak start date, your "why," daily check-ins, journal entries, relapse notes, badges, settings) in your browser or phone's local storage, on your device. This data never leaves your device. We have no server that receives it, and no way to read it. Deleting the app data (or using "erase everything" in settings) removes it permanently.

The 60-second check (quiz)

Your quiz answers are scored inside your browser by the page itself. They are not transmitted, stored, or seen by us.

What we do NOT do

  • No accounts, no email collection by the app or this site
  • No analytics or tracking pixels on the app
  • No selling, sharing, or transferring of personal data (we hold none to sell)
  • No notifications or messages unless you enable them on your device (reminders are generated locally)

What third parties handle

Payments and membership run through Whop (whop.com). When you start a trial or subscribe, Whop collects your payment and account details under Whop's own privacy policy and terms. We see membership status, not your card details.

The community also lives on Whop. Anything you choose to post there is visible to other members under whatever display name you pick. Many members use an alias. We recommend it.

Referral tags: if you arrive through a partner link (for example a link with a source tag), this site remembers that tag in your own browser's local storage and passes it to Whop at checkout so we know which partner sent you. It identifies the partner, not you.

Hosting: this site is served as static files by GitHub Pages, which may log standard technical request data (like IP addresses) as any web host does.

Age

GoonBuster is intended for users 16 and older. Purchasing a membership requires meeting Whop's age requirements.

Not medical advice

GoonBuster is a self-improvement tool, not medical care, therapy, or a substitute for professional help. If you are struggling, talking to a professional is a strong move, not a weak one.

Changes and contact

If this policy changes, the update appears on this page with a new effective date. Questions: reach us inside the GoonBuster community on Whop.
